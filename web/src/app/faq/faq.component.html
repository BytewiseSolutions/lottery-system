1. Is TotalFreeLotto really free to play?

+

Absolutely—TotalFreeLotto is 100% free. You don't need to pay anything to enter any of our draws. There are no hidden fees, no subscription charges, and no in-app purchases. You simply pick your numbers and submit your entry. We believe everyone deserves a fair chance to win without ever having to spend their hard-earned money.

2. How does TotalFreeLotto make money if players don't pay?

+

Our platform is supported entirely through advertising partnerships. When people visit TotalFreeLotto and engage with the site, we earn revenue from ads shown by trusted platforms like Google AdSense and PropellerAds. This revenue helps keep the game free and also funds our prize pools. So, by playing, you help grow the winnings—without ever paying a cent.

3. Do winners actually receive real money?

+

Yes! When you win, you win real, spendable money. Every draw has a prize pool that grows based on entries, and winners receive payouts from that pool. Our system is designed to be fair, transparent, and reliable, and we take pride in honoring every legitimate win.

4. How is the winning amount calculated?

+

The prize pool depends on how many total entries are received for a draw. For every single entry submitted, we allocate $0.001 to the pool. So, the more people play, the bigger the prize becomes. This model keeps the system sustainable while giving players the fun of watching the prize grow as the countdown to the draw gets closer.

5. How do winners get paid?

+

We want to make receiving your winnings as easy and convenient as possible. Once a player wins, they can choose a payment method that works for them. We currently support PayPal, Mukuru, Western Union, MoneyGram, and other widely used global payout methods. This ensures that players from different parts of the world can receive their money securely and without hassle.

6. What happens if there are multiple winners?

+

If more than one person wins a draw, the prize pool is shared equally among all winners. This keeps the process fair and transparent. Whether you're the only winner or one of many, you'll always receive your rightful share of the prize money.

7. How do I play TotalFreeLotto?

+

Playing is simple and fun. Each entry requires you to choose 5 numbers from the first set of 99 numbers and 2 bonus numbers from a second set of 99 numbers. If all your chosen numbers match the winning numbers, you will win the prize pool!

8. When and where are the draws held?

+

We host three live draws every week—on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. Draws typically take place at around 21:00 South Africa Standard Time, though this may change in the future. To keep everything transparent, the draws are conducted live by our CEO and streamed on platforms such as Facebook, YouTube, or TikTok so players can watch the results unfold in real time.

9. What features can I use on the website?

+

TotalFreeLotto offers two main features designed to make your playing experience smarter and easier:

  • Results: View winning numbers and number of winners without even logging in.
  • History: A personal collection of all the entries you've made so you can keep track of your numbers.

These tools help you stay organized and understand your progress over time.
